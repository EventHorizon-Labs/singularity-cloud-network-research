# Singularity Cloud Network Paper Series

This roadmap keeps the paper series coherent as new manuscripts are added.

## SCN-001: Tokenized Compute Markets

Status: released as working paper v0.1.

Core question:

How can a compute network finance its own physical capacity without central capital allocation?

Primary contribution:

A mechanism that converts market activity into a Hardware Fund, couples tokenized settlement to real inference demand, and states the failure modes that must be controlled.

## SCN-002: Singularity Grid

Status: forthcoming.

Expected scope:

- decentralized inference network architecture
- node identity, staking, job routing, and reputation
- end-to-end encrypted prompt delivery
- attestation and verifiable result flows
- operator economics and settlement

## SCN-003: Singularity Hive Mind

Status: forthcoming.

Expected scope:

- large-model serving across multiple independent machines
- model partitioning and scheduling
- latency hiding over consumer and cloud links
- failure recovery, verification, and economic contribution accounting
- relationship to Singularity Grid and Tokenized Compute Markets

## Naming

Use `SCN-###` for papers in this repository. Experiments in the broader lab repository may also use `SCN-###`; when a paper grows out of an experiment, link the experiment from the paper README.
