# arXiv Submission Notes

This note tracks likely arXiv categories for the current EventHorizon Labs research papers.

## Tokenized Compute Markets

Best-fit categories to consider:

- `cs.GT` Computer Science and Game Theory: strongest technical fit if the submission is framed around mechanism design, incentives, market design, and agentic market creation.
- `econ.TH` Theoretical Economics: plausible if the submission is framed as economic mechanism design / market design rather than distributed systems.
- `cs.DC` Distributed, Parallel, and Cluster Computing: relevant for the compute-grid systems layer, but less central than the market mechanism in the current manuscript.
- `cs.CY` Computers and Society: fallback if the paper is framed around social/economic impact of decentralized AI infrastructure, though it is less precise.
- `q-fin.EC` Economics: possible cross-list if the emphasis becomes token markets, market microstructure, and settlement economics.

Recommended framing for first submission attempt:

1. Primary: `cs.GT`
2. Cross-list: `cs.DC` if endorsement permits
3. Alternative primary if CS endorsement is blocked: `econ.TH`

The title and abstract should emphasize "mechanism design", "market design", "incentive compatibility", "settlement", and "agentic compute markets" if submitting outside `cs.DC`.
